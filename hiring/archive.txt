
        <h3 id="producer"><a href="#producer">Producer / Business Development Coordinator</a></h3>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; ">

            <li>Coordinate documentation, communications, and deliverables for grants or funding bodies</li>
            <li>Coordinate remote video meetings with all team members on a regular cadence to ensure cohesiveness in workflows</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; "><b>Requirements:</b></p>
        <ul class="bullet-list" style="margin-top: 20px; font-weight: normal; ">
            <li>Strong organizational, communication, and leadership skills</li>
            <li>Familiarity with team tools such as Trello, Jira, or equivalent project management software</li>
        </ul>


        <h3 id="3d-vfx-generalist"><a href="#3d-vfx-generalist">3D/VFX Generalist (Environments-Focused)</a></h3>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; ">
            We are looking for a 3D/VFX Generalist with strong artistic instincts and a good sense of atmosphere to help
            shape the world of Bushcraft Survival. You'll be responsible for creating high-quality environmental assets and VFX that contribute to
            the grounded and immersive wilderness experience. While technical art support is available, your work should be clean and game-ready.
            Although you will not be performing full art direction duties, we are really looking for someone who wants to step up and provide voice
            and clarity to the game's 3D art direction. You will be coordinating your visual artistic ideas with the UI designer.
        </p>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; "><b>Responsibilities:</b></p>
        <ul class="bullet-list" style="margin-top: 20px; font-weight: normal; ">
            <li>Create 3D models, textures, and materials for terrain, foliage, rocks, and handcrafted world assets (Blender experience a plus, but other software licenses can be accommodated)</li>
            <li>Design and implement atmospheric VFX such as fire, weather, wind, and ambient effects, with a focus on cold, snow-covered environments</li>
            <li>Work with the lead game developer to ensure assets are pipelined properly (Godot experience a plus)</li>
            <li>Have general shader knowledge on how to achieve real-time VFX effects that are performant</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; "><b>Requirements:</b></p>
        <ul class="bullet-list" style="margin-top: 20px; font-weight: normal; ">
            <li>A strong portfolio showcasing real-time environment art and/or stylized realism</li>
            <li>Proficiency in any 3D software, as well as experience implementing assets in Unreal, Unity or Godot</li>
        </ul>


        <h3 id="ui-ux-designer"><a href="#ui-ux-designer">UI/UX Designer</a></h3>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; ">As the UI designer, you will design intuitive and immersive features that do everything
            to prevent the player from being taken out of the experience of Bushcraft Survival. You will produce interface mockups,
            images and icons to be used in game, as well as vector art. The art direction for the UI is moody, rustic, and wilderness.
            Although you will not be performing full art direction duties, we are really looking for someone who wants to step up and provide voice
            and clarity to the game's 2D art direction. You will be coordinating your visual artistic ideas with the 3D/VFX designer.
        </p>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; "><b>Responsiblities:</b></p>
        <ul class="bullet-list" style="margin-top: 20px; font-weight: normal; ">
            <li>Design UI/UX for first-person survival game mechanics, inventory, crafting, HUD, and more</li>
            <li>Collaborate with game designer to produce high-level UI mockups that match player flow and art direction</li>
            <li>Create vector and raster images to be incorporated in-game</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="margin-top: 20px; "><b>Requirements:</b></p>
        <ul class="bullet-list" style="margin-top: 20px; font-weight: normal; ">
            <li>A strong portfolio showing complate game UI designs and implementations, custom asset design</li>
            <li>Work experience with UX (User Experience) design, including optimizing gameplay controls, mechanic design, and accessibility interfaces</li>
        </ul>
